Vegetable Salad

Description & Importance:

Vegetable salad is one of the healthiest food choices. It is made from fresh vegetables such as lettuce, tomatoes , cucumbers, and carrots. This type of salad is low in calories and rich in fiber, which helps improve digestion and maintain a healthy weight. Eating vegetable salad regularly supports the immune system and keeps the body hydrated.

Tips

Use fresh vegetables

Avoid creamy dressings

Add olive oil and lemon juice




Nutrition Facts(per 100 g) vitamins
Calories 25 kcal A
Protein 1.5 g C
Fat 0.3 g Potassium
Carbohydrates 5 g Folate

Chicken Salad

Description & Importance:

Chicken salad is a nutritious meal that combines lean protein with fresh vegetables. It helps build muscles and provides long-lasting energy. Grilled chicken is the best option because it is lower in fat. Chicken salad is ideal for people who want a healthy and filling meal.

Tips

Use grilled chicken

Avoid mayonnaise

Add leafy greens




Nutrition Facts(per 100 g) vitamins
Calories 150 kcal B6
Protein 20 g Phosphorus
Fat 7 g Iron
Carbohydrates 3 g

Fruit Salad

Description & Importance:

Fruit salad is a refreshing and healthy dish made from a mix of different fruits. It provides natural sugars, vitamins, and antioxidants that give the body energy. Fruit salad also helps improve digestion and supports skin health. It is best eaten fresh without added sugar.

Tips

Use seasonal fruits

Avoid added sugar

Serve fresh




Nutrition Facts(per 100 g) vitamins
Calories 60 kcal C
Protein 1 g A
Fat 0.2 g Potassium
Carbohydrates 15 g

Tuna Salad

Description & Importance:

Tuna salad is a protein-rich meal that supports heart and brain health. It contains omega-3 fatty acids, which are important for the body. Tuna salad is light, nutritious, and suitable for people following a healthy diet, especially when prepared without mayonnaise.

Tips

Use water-packed tuna

Avoid mayonnaise

Add lemon and olive oil




Nutrition Facts(per 100 g) vitamins
Calories 130 kcal Omega-3
Protein 22 g D
Fat 4 g B12
Carbohydrates 2 g Selenium

Healthy Food

Healthy food plays a vital role in maintaining both physical and mental well-being. It is not just about eating to satisfy hunger, but about nourishing the body with essential nutrients that help it function properly. A healthy diet provides the energy needed for daily activities, supports the immune system, improves concentration, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases. Eating healthy means consuming a balanced variety of foods that include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Fruits and vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that protect the body from illness and strengthen immunity. Whole grains such as oats, brown rice, and whole wheat bread provide fiber, which aids digestion and helps maintain stable blood sugar levels.
